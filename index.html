About Me

I believe what makes me stand out amongst the crowd is my passion and dedication towards learning and understanding topics. I take pride in what I do and my grit towards accomplishing any given tasks is something that makes me a reliable member of the team.

This passion carried me through at Ohlone College with a smooth transition to the California Polytechnic University at San Luis Obispo. At Ohlone College, I took higher-level math courses like the Calculus series, Differential Equations, Linear Algebra, and Statistics. Apart from Math, I completed all my Engineering courses which include Circuits Analysis, Materials, Statics alongside Calculus-based Physics.

With a profound STEM background, I have also taken all the major Business courses such as Financial & Managerial Accounting, Micro & Macro-Economics, Introduction to Computing Business, and other related courses that have broadened my scope of business and have helped me apply those skills to the real work field.

Overall, excelling in these classes make me reliable enough to be a valuable asset for any engineering projects and/or business-related opportunities that may come up in the future.

If my profile sparks an interest, please feel free to reach out to me.
